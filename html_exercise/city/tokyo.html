Tokyo

Tokyo, the capital of Japan, is one of the most populous metropolitan areas in the world. Known for its cutting-edge technology and efficiency, Tokyo also preserves deep-rooted traditions such as tea ceremonies, sumo wrestling, and temple visits. It's a place where skyscrapers stand beside centuries-old shrines, symbolizing the harmonious coexistence of past and future.
