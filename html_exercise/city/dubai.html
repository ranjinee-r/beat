Dubai

Once a small trading port, Dubai has rapidly transformed into a luxurious global city. Famous for its futuristic skyline, man-made islands, and towering Burj Khalifa, Dubai is a testament to visionary urban planning and economic diversification. It's also becoming a center for tourism, technology, and innovation in the Middle East.
