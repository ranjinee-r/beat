New York

A global icon of ambition and diversity, New York City is a dynamic urban landscape filled with opportunity. From Wall Street’s financial powerhouse to Broadway’s dazzling theatre scene, NYC attracts dreamers from all corners of the globe. Its five boroughs are home to more than 8 million people, representing over 180 nationalities and languages.
